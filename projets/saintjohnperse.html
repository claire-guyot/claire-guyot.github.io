Saint-John Perse

//

Images à Crusoé, recueil du poète Saint-John Perse est un livre qui me touche, qui m’inspire, par sa profondeur et la beauté de ses textes. Cette affiche vise à lui rendre hommage, tout en m’ayant permis certaines expérimentations photographiques.

Images for Crusoe, a collection of poesy by Saint-John Perse, a french author, is a book that inspires me, with his depth and the beauty of his texts. So, with this poster, I wanted to pay hommage to him, and realize some photographic experimentations in the same time.
